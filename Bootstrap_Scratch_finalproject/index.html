Scratch

Scratch out the fear of eating.

Start your journey

Reducing your risk, just a click away.

Scratch is the first leading food allergy guide pioneered by college students for college students. The search engine is tailored to your geographical location to provide restaurants that cater to specific dietary restrictions. Dedicated to maintaining accurate, up-to-date information on the safest and most accomodating restaurants in college towns across the country, we also provide a forum so users can connect and grade restaurants based on their dining experiences.
